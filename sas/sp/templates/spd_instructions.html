Athletics Eligibility Certification Instructions

Inbox

Satisfactory Progress (SP) and Percentage of Degree (PD) forms are classified according to their status in the system.

Form created:
Athletic Student Services has generated the forms for this semester and is reviewing the major and course info before sending it to the colleges.
Needs college attention:
It is the College's (or designees' )turn to verify either verify the courses on the SP form or to calculate a percentage of degree on the PD form.
Approved by college:
The College (or designee) has signed off on the form and Athletic Student Services is reviewing the changes.
Requires Registrar approval:
It is the Registrar's (or designee's) turn to verify the courses and grades on the form and sign off.
Complete:
The form has been signed off by Athletic Student Serviecs, the College, and the Registrar's Office.
Form inactive:
That form for that student/major combination will not be used that semester. The student may have another form of the same type listed under a different major.

Satisfactory Progress Form

INSTRUCTIONS: The Athletic Student Services staff in the Athletics Department will provide forms with Field of Study, Course Number, Unique, and Credit Hours for a given semester. The Colleges (or designees) are responsible for completing the Countable, Min. Grade Required, and Pass/Fail Accepted columns. The Registrar (or designee) is responsible for verifying that the listed major/minor is consistent with the student's official record. After final grades post, the Registrar is responsible for verifying completed coursework, final grades, and total countable degree hours.

(ATHLETIC STUDENT SERVICES) Verify student's current major, minor, catalog, coursework, and credit hours. Can also delete/inactive the form as needed.

(COLLEGES) Indicate if the course counts toward the major/minor listed upon successful completion of the coursework. Answer yes if the course is countable and no if it is not.

(COLLEGES) If there is a minimum grade required for the course to count toward the student's degree, indicate the minimum grade required using the letter grades (B-, C, C-, D, etc.) provided in the dropdown box.

(COLLEGES) If the course is being taken on a pass/fail basis, indicate if a pass counts toward the degree. Answer yes if a pass counts and no if it does not.

(REGISTRAR) Certify the final grade at the end of the semester.

(REGISTRAR) Certify the final hours for each class successfully completed.

(REGISTRAR) Certify total countable degree hours for the semester.

Fulfillment of Percentage of Degree Requirements

INSTRUCTIONS: The Athletic Student Services staff in the Athletics Department will provide forms with major information. The Colleges (or designees) are responsible for the projected countable hours and total hours required fields. A degree audit is run in advance and the field is prepopulated, but the Colleges (or designees) can alter the hours if the degree audit is not inclusive of all a student's work. The projected percentage will be calculated. The Registrar (or designee) is responsible for verifying that the listed major/minor is consistent with the student's official record.

(ATHLETIC STUDENT SERVICES) Verify student's current major, minor, and catalog. Can also delete/inactive the form as needed,or create an additional form for a different major.

(COLLEGES) Enter and verify the projected countable hours required for the student's degree.

(COLLEGES) Enter and verify the total hours required for the student's degree.

(REGISTRAR) Certify the projected countable hours, total hours required, and projected percentage at the end of the semester.

Manage Users

INSTRUCTIONS: The Athletic Student Services staff and Registrar's staff can authorize new users for the system and remove users.

To add a user, enter an EID into the form at the top of the page. If the user in a College, the school must also be selected.

To remove a user, unselect the 'active' button in the row with the user's name and hit 'Update'.
